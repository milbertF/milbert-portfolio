Self-Portrait Digital Collage

Title: Defiance Amidst Chaos

This digital collage tells a powerful story of courage and resiliency. Three fighter jets fly overhead, displaying strength and resolve against a background of flaming explosions and foreboding skies. The figure at the front is calm and unwavering, wearing tactical clothing that represents bravery and preparedness. Despite the turmoil, his crossed arms and unblinking gaze exude an unbreakable soul. An aura of passion and defiance is created by the harmonious blending of the rich orange, red, and yellow tones with the delicate accents of smoke and sparks. The composition is anchored by the name "MILBERT," which is written in bold, flaming font, implying a personal connection to the scene. Action, strength, and self-identity are all expertly combined in this striking piece of art.

Advocacy Poster

Title: Choose Green Today: Let Our Nature Stay

An effective visual call to action for environmental preservation is this advocacy poster. In the middle of a verdant forest, the image shows the back of a lone person carrying a hiking rucksack. The strong font overlay, "CHOOSE GREEN TODAY, LET OUR NATURE STAY," highlights how vital it is to take action for the future of the earth. Because of its transparency, the typography symbolises harmony between nature and humanity by letting the beauty of the forest shine through. Natural lighting and vivid green hues are used to emphasise the peace and significance of protecting natural settings. An encouraging reminder of each person's role in creating a sustainable and environmentally friendly planet is provided by this piece of art.

Wordmark

Title: SIENNE: She Has Global Impact

Named after the French word for "her," "Sienne" is a natural fit for the brand's mission: to celebrate and empower women who make a difference in the world. The logo design was created to honour and motivate women worldwide and is a visual ode to strength, femininity, and leadership. The design's focal point is a graceful woman's silhouette, which stands for grace, confidence, and independence. The wordmark "Sienne," which is presented in a powerful serif font that strikes a mix between modernism and classic elegance, goes with the silhouette. The silhouette is included into the "e" to emphasise the importance of women in the brand's story. Under the wordmark, the tagline "She Has Global Impact" reaffirms the brand's mission to honour women's efforts across the globe.
